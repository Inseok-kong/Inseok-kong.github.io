Inseok Kong

University of Seoul | 3D Vision, Robust Learning, Test-Time Adaptation

Seoul, Republic of Korea kong796336 at gmail.com

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Seoul (UOS), majoring in Geo-Informatics with a double major in Artificial Intelligence.

I am currently an undergraduate research student in the Robotics and Computer Vision Lab, advised by Prof. Jiyoung Jung. My research interests lie in Test-Time Adaptation, Domain Generalization, and Robust Learning under distribution shifts, especially for 3D vision and vision-language settings.

I am interested in developing models that adapt reliably to unseen environments and remain robust under corruptions, adversarial perturbations, and real-world domain shifts.
