I am an undergraduate student at the **University of Seoul (UOS)**, majoring in **Geo-Informatics** with a double major in **Artificial Intelligence**.

I am a research intern at the **Statistical Inference and Information Theory Lab at KAIST EE**, having previously worked as an undergraduate research student in the Robotics and Computer Vision Lab at UOS under the supervision of Prof. Jiyoung Jung. My research focus is on building machine learning models that adapt reliably to unseen environments and remain robust under corruptions, adversarial perturbations, and real-world domain shifts. I am particularly interested in **Test-Time Adaptation** and **Domain Generalization**. Recently, I have also been highly motivated to explore research in **Vision-Language Models (VLMs)** and **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** models.
